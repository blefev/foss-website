Why use FOSS?

The internet as you know it would be impossible without free and open source software. The world's most critical infrastructure relies heavily on libre (another term for free and open) software. The large majority of top (non-Microsoft-owned) internet sites run on Linux. Most of the computers in the world, if we include phones, run Linux. FOSS software isn't just a name - it's a philosophy. You should have the right to know what programs are running on hardware you own. Furthermore, you should have the right to inspect those programs and modify them to your heart's content.

How to get started with FOSS

Getting started with FOSS can seem daunting - but you've already begun!! Many components of your operating system, your router, your ISP's routers, and the server giving you this web page are already using free and open source software. If you want to further your journey of liberation, why not try out one of many Linux distributions?
