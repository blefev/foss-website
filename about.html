About

This Site

This website is devoted to all things related to free and open source software. But we aren't just in it for the source code. We believe in the right of knowing what's in the software you run on your personal hardware. Proprietary software lends itself to poor coding, infestations of backdoors, and anything else that can be hidden by a compiler.

Our Goal

The world has been dominated by proprietary software corporations. These companies helped push the computing industry to the mainstream, but people remain in ignorance over the ways their data is being reaped, often without their real consent. The FOSS movement has gained significaant traction in the past decade, and Linux marketshare is growing faster than ever before. Now is the best time for people to educate themselves on the benefits of FOSS. This site aims to provide this education, and vanquish common fear, uncertainty, and doubt about FOSS.

How You Can Contribute

We encourage you to donate to the Free Software Foundation so they can continue their work of imbuing the benefits of FOSS across the globe. Otherwise, the best way to help is to contribute to FOSS projects. You can do this in several ways:

  • Download and use FOSS
  • Give developers feedback on your user experience
  • Tell your friends and family about FOSS
  • Contribute code to FOSS projects.
